Belgian Economic Policy Uncertainty

The charts below show the daily, respectively monthly average number of news articles on economic policy uncertainty, published by Flemish news papers. The news articles that refer to policy uncertainty are automatically detected using a prediction model created by the Applied Data Mining research team at the University of Antwerp. The resulting index is a proxy of economic policy uncertainty as perceived by Flemish media.

The daily values of the index can be downloaded from this site and can be used for research purposes. A reference list with related research will be kept, so feel free to send us a reference to the paper that includes your analyses using this data. Please select a day in the top chart to see the highest ranked article of the day, i.e. the article with the most references to policy uncertainty. The word cloud at the bottom of the page represents the terms that appear most frequently in the news articles on policy uncertainty during the selected year or day.

More information on the methodology can be found in our working paper.
